## What's new

### 1. Individual Mode — Self-Paced Challenges

Participants can now work through challenges independently at their own pace, with their progress saved automatically.

**How it works:**

- A participant visits the challenge page and sees the full description, sprint overview, and structure — no barriers.
- To begin, they enter their email at the bottom of the page and click **Start Challenge**.
- They are taken to the sprint dashboard showing all sprints. Sprint 1 is unlocked; subsequent sprints are locked.
- The participant works through assignments in Sprint 1. Each completion is tracked with green checkmarks and progress bars.
- When all assignments in a sprint are complete, the next sprint unlocks automatically.
- On return visits, the participant goes straight to their sprint dashboard with all progress intact — no need to re-enter their email.

**How to test it:**

1. Open the preview URL and navigate to `/c/leadership-essentials`
2. You'll see the "Leadership Essentials" challenge landing page with:
   - "Self-paced" badge, sprint count, and "Sequential unlock" indicator
   - A "What you'll cover" section listing all 3 sprints
   - An email form at the bottom
3. Enter any email address (e.g. `test@example.com`) and click **Start Challenge**
4. You'll be redirected to the sprint dashboard:
   - Sprint 1 "Communication Foundations" is unlocked (3 assignments)
   - Sprint 2 "Decision Making" is locked
   - Sprint 3 "Team Building" is locked
5. Click Sprint 1, open each assignment, read it — the content is real leadership development material
6. After viewing assignments, progress should be tracked (check marks, progress bar in header)
7. Close the browser, reopen the preview URL, navigate back to `/c/leadership-essentials` — you should see "Continue Your Progress" instead of the email form

**Demo challenge created for testing:**
- **Leadership Essentials** — 3 sprints, 8 assignments total
  - Sprint 1: Communication Foundations (Active Listening, Giving Constructive Feedback, Presentation Skills)
  - Sprint 2: Decision Making (The Decision Matrix, Cognitive Biases in Leadership)
  - Sprint 3: Team Building (Psychological Safety, Delegation That Develops, Running Effective Retrospectives)

### 2. Preview/Staging Workflow

We have implemented a staging workflow so that no changes go directly to production.

- All development now happens on a separate branch
- Each update generates a unique preview URL on Vercel
- The preview URL can be shared for review and testing
- Only after approval do changes get merged to production
- Your live site is never affected by work-in-progress
